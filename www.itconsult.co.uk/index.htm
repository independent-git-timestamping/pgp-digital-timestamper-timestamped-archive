I. T. Consultancy Limited

I. T. Consultancy Limited is based in Jersey in the Channel Islands and provides independent advice and practical assistance, to its clients, in the areas of information technology and communications. In order to ensure its independence, it sells no goods of any sort and derives its whole income from time based fees.

It operates Stamper, a free PGP digital timestamping service incorporating email Proof of Posting Certificates.

It also provides an email pinging service which will return any message received to the sender including full headers. This might be useful for testing email systems.
